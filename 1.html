CSE Department

The CSE department focuses on providing high-quality education in computer science, programming, and IT-related fields.

EEE Department

The EEE department offers programs in electrical and electronic engineering, preparing students for careers in energy and power systems.

ECE Department

The ECE department specializes in electronics, communication, and signal processing, with a focus on research and development.

Mechanical Engineering

The MECH department offers comprehensive education in mechanical engineering, preparing students for a variety of industries.

Civil Engineering

The Civil department focuses on construction, infrastructure, and environmental engineering to shape the future of urban development.

AIML Department

The AIML department offers cutting-edge courses in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to prepare students for modern tech careers.

MBA Department

The MBA department provides advanced knowledge in business administration, leadership, and management for future business leaders.

ECT Department

The ECT department offers courses related to electronics and communication technologies, including wireless communication systems.

Girls' Hostel

The girls' hostel offers safe and comfortable living spaces with all necessary facilities and security for female students.

Playground

The campus has extensive playground facilities for outdoor sports like football, cricket, and more.

Bus Area

Dedicated area for buses that transport students from the college to various locations.
